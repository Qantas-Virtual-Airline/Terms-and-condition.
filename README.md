# Terms-and-condition.
By reading this you accept our terms and condition.

Welcome to the official Discord for ATC24, VATSIM, and the Qantas Virtual Airline. This server is a professional aviation simulation environment. By staying here, you agree to follow all rules below.

:pilot: GENERAL CONDUCT
Be respectful and professional at all times. No harassment, discrimination, hate speech, threats, or toxic behaviour. No drama or arguments. Treat others as you would in a real aviation workplace. Staff decisions are final.

:no_entry_sign: Zero tolerance for racism, sexism, homophobia, or targeted harassment.


:airplane_departure: AVIATION PROFESSIONALISM
This server follows real-world aviation standards. Keep discussions aviation-focused and realistic. No unrealistic behaviour or disruption during operations. Use ICAO phraseology where applicable. ATC authority must be respected during live sessions.

:warning: Disrupting ATC operations may result in immediate action.

:globe_with_meridians: VATSIM & ATC24 RULES
All members must follow the VATSIM Code of Conduct, User Agreement, and ATC24 guidelines. No trolling, fake emergencies, frequency abuse, or impersonation of staff or ATC. Serious violations may be reported to VATSIM.

:kangaroo: QANTAS VIRTUAL AIRLINE
Use Qantas callsigns (QFA / QF) correctly. No misuse of Qantas branding. Follow approved routes, SOPs, and training requirements. No false flight or hour logging.

:speech_balloon: DISCORD & VOICE RULES
No spam, NSFW content, politics, advertising, or excessive swearing.
Voice channels require push-to-talk, no background noise, no soundboards, and English only during official operations. Treat voice like real radio frequencies.

:shield: STAFF & ENFORCEMENT
Follow staff instructions. Do not argue moderation publicly. Use tickets for applications, support, or appeals. Staff impersonation results in an instant ban.

:scales: Enforcement: Warning → Timeout → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban
Severe breaches may skip steps.

:airplane: FINAL NOTICE
This is a realistic aviation simulation community. If you’re here to fly professionally and respect the network — welcome aboard.
